About me

My name is Nasreen Namuz and I am an interior designer

The stories that are woven in my studio are not about the houses that are designed - but about the connection between people, their personalities and the spaces they want to live in.

The process invested in getting to know my customers, their experiences and preferences, is the one that ultimately produces a customized and unforgettable experience for every space!

My studio opened in 2017

During this period, I managed to work in front of a special amount of people, make them excited, and feel that they have fulfilled their dreams...
